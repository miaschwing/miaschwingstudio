About Me

Hello! My name is Mia Schwinghammer. I am a young, self-taught artist who has been drawing since I was a child and started pushing my love for art past being a hobby in 2020. I have since gained a deep passion for sharing my art with others in the forms of murals, prints, and stickers.

Contact

I am always looking for new ways to collaborate with people through commissions or working with other artists. Wether you would like a silly sticker or a larger mural don't hesitate to email me or fill out my contact form below!
